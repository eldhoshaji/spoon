We depend on credit for major purchases in life. What about credit for our everyday purchase? We often borrow money only to deal with the embarrassment of an IOU or we borrow money on our credit card. The credit available on a card is available only to a person with a bank account and a salary that can afford the credit limit. This leaves out young millennials who are yet to be employed but need credit. That's where we step in- to provide micro credit at the click of a download. Spoon aims to provide credit to everyone- regardless of their income or age. It provides millennials with a chance to build their CIBIL score for future loan requirements and an oppurtunity to save and utilize money effectively.

We're powered by some of the top banks and NBFC's to facilitate the transfer of credit. Also, we're associated with some of the top e-tailors, merchants to transfer payment instantly. The end result? Quick hassie free credit coupled with interest free repayment leading to consumers worldwide
